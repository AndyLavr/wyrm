2.0 - Alerts

Located in wyrm_core/_alert.sass. Alerts can exist as in page notices and as fixed drops.

<div class="wy-alert {$modifiers}">
 <div class="wy-alert-title">Title goes here</div>
 <p>Alert instructions go here.</p>
</div>

  • Default styling without modifier classes.

    Title goes here

    Alert instructions go here.
  • .wy-alert-neutral

    Generic alert, extra instruction.
    Title goes here

    Alert instructions go here.
  • .wy-alert-success

    Hooray, something good happened.
    Title goes here

    Alert instructions go here.
  • .wy-alert-warning

    Watch out, you might make a mistake.
    Title goes here

    Alert instructions go here.
  • .wy-alert-danger

    Yep, you made a mistake.
