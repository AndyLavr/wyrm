7.0 - Spinners

Spinners are pretty simple. You can change the color by changing $spinner-color. Also, any of the modifiers below can also carry .wy-spinner-absolute to position the spinner in the absolute center of your wrapping area.

<div class="wy-spinner {$modifiers}"></div>
<a href="#random" class="btn"><span class="wy-spinner {$modifiers}"></span> Loading</a>
